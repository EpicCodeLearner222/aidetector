- Hey is an informal greeting often used to call attention, express surprise, or simply say hello in a relaxed way.
- "Hey" is commonly spoken among friends or acquaintances and has become a standard casual way to start a conversation in person or via text.​
- It can signal excitement, a question, or just a prompt for interaction, such as "Hey, how are you?" or "Hey, listen to this!".
- The term "HEY" is also used for an email service that aims to rethink how users handle emails and personal communications, prioritizing control and privacy.​
- "Hey" appears in many song titles and lyrics, including works by the Backstreet Boys and Outkast, emphasizing its popularity in media and entertainment.​​
